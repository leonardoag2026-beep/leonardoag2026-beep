### 3D Printing & Modeling

I've been doing 3D modeling and printing for some time now, and I usually like to make practical custom parts myself. I do parametric modeling with OpenCAD and create organic shapes and art-style models with Blender. I'm very familiar with G code, and I write it myself, read it and fine-tune the speed, the quality and the behavior of the material. I also built the FarmLoop Stage 2 on the Bambu Lab A1 Mini using a Raspberry Pi Pico and designed and built the case and mechanical parts myself. I have worked with a variety of filaments, including PLA, PETG and TPU, and I have a complete understanding of how to adjust each material. Even if the STL file gets corrupted, it can be repaired, and the results can change dramatically with a few changes to the slicing settings.
